Hewlett Packard Enterprise in 2016 launched HPE Haven OnDemand, an innovative cloud platform that provides advanced machine learning APIs and services. The platform enables developers, startups and enterprises to build data-rich mobile and enterprise applications. Its Advanced Text Analysis helps excerpt the key meaning from language by employing powerful concept extraction capabilities that go beyond traditional approaches to obtain key concepts, entities, and sentiment from text sources. Delivered as a service on Microsoft Azure, HPE Haven OnDemand provides more than 60 APIs and services that deliver deep learning analytics on a wide range of data.
